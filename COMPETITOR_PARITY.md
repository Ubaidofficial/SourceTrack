# Competitor Parity

Guardrails for what SourceTrack can and cannot claim vs competitors.
Do not claim parity without verified capture, storage, UI, QA, and docs.

## DataFast

Public docs show:
- UTM tracking fields
- Campaign analytics API (utm fields, ref/source/via, visitors, revenue)
- Revenue attribution / payment provider documentation
- Meta ads attribution documentation

Do not claim DataFast parity until:
- Campaign API/export parity verified
- Revenue attribution verified end-to-end
- Payment-provider attribution verified
- Ad attribution verified

## Cometly

Positions around: marketing attribution, ad performance, ROI proof, conversion sync, multi-touch attribution, customer journey, ad campaign optimization.

Do not claim Cometly parity until:
- Ad spend/campaign/adset/ad IDs supported
- Conversion sync exists
- Multi-touch beyond current models verified
- Journey view is productized
- Offline/CRM events verified

## Usermaven

Positions around: full customer journey, marketing attribution, product analytics, ecommerce analytics, retention/lifecycle.

Do not claim Usermaven parity until:
- Product analytics events productized
- Ecommerce dashboards are real-data backed
- Retention/lifecycle analytics exist
- Journey view is complete

## Do-not-overclaim list

Do not claim support for:
- Business-specific dashboards (backend done, frontend not built)
- Full Cometly / DataFast / Usermaven parity
- Ad spend attribution
- Conversion sync
- MRR / CAC / ROAS / CPL / payback
- CRM sync
- Universal AI traffic detection

unless code, data, and QA confirm it.
